THE FAMOUS TOURISM SITES IN BARCELONA

The Sagrada Familia is a Cathelic church designed by Antoni Gaudi in the year 1852-1926. This tourism site is one of the most historic sites in Barcelona, which is exactly a 140 years old Cathelic church. This historical architechure is recognised as the largest unfinished Cathelic church in the whole world.

La Rambla is the most well known street in Central Barcelona. This steet streches 1.2 kilometers connecting the Placa de Catalunya. This street has the monument of Christoper Columbus on the center of the street. Christopher Columbus is an explorer who discovered America and is one of Spain's prides. This street is a multicultural mix with a hub of history and culture.

The Barcelona Cathedral is a major tourisum site in Barcelona also known as Cathedral of Holy Cross and Saint Euilalia. This Cathedral was constructed in the thirteenth to fifteenth centuries, showing that this is also one of the most historic tourism sites in Barcelona.

The Picasso Museum is an art museum that is one of the popular torisum sites in Barcelona Catalonia. As the name says Picasso, this museum is a museum dedicated to all the artwork created by a world famous artist from Barcelona, Pablo Picasso who is most popular for his cubism artworks such as the Guitar artwork that is popular worldwide.
