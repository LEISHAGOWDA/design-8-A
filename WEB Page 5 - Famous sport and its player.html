THE FAMOUS SPORT AND ITS FAMOUS PLAYER

 
 

BARCELONA FOOTBALL CLUB - FCB

Barcelona is famous for its famous sport, Football that is the sport that is widely played across Barcelona. The Barcelona football club, Futbol Club Barcelona most popularly recognised as FC Barcelona is one of the top 5 football clubs that holds the top 4 position. Joan Gamper, a Swiss man first found the Barcelona Football Club, FC Barcelona on 29th November 1899. The FC Barcelona Club has played about 618 matches and won 349 matches  according to latest data. Footballers such as Robert Lewandowski, Lionel Messi, Neymar, Charles Puyol, Gerard Pique, etc are a few footballers who have played for the FC Barcelona Club, Especially Lionel Messi and Robert Lewandowski are two of the most famous footballlers in this club.

BARCELONA FAMOUS FOOTBALLER - ROBERT LEWANDOWSKI

Robert Lewandowski is famous successfull footballer who played for FC Barcelona club and is also yet playing for the same. Born on 21st August 1988 Warsaw, Poland, who is currently thirty five years old. Lewandowski is a Polish professional footballer, who first joint the barcelona club on 19th july 2022. He plays as a striker in the La Liga Barcelona club and captains the Poland national team, who is regarded as one of the best strikers of all time. This famous footballer has scored fifty six goals when playing for the FC Barcelona club.
