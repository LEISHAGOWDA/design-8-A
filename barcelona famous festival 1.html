THE FAMOUS FESTIVALS OF BARCELONA 

 

Dia de Sant Jordi is the most important festivals of Barcelona. This festival of Barcelona is celebrated on the 23rd of April every year and this festival can also be called the highlight of April in Barcelona. The day of Dia de Sant Jordi celebrates the Patron Saint of Barcelona, Eulalia, who was a 13 year old Roman Christian virgin known for being oppressed in the city of Barcelona during the time when the Christians where persecuted in the reign of emperor diocletian. On this day, the whole of Las Ramblas, a well known street in Central Barcelona is made into a flower street. The reason behind transforming this street into a flower stall is that men should show their appreciation for women through these flowers as a act of going against Eulalia's case in the past.
