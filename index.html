A Brief in Spanish

Barcelona is the capital city of Catalonia in Spain. Barcelona has a population of 5,687,000 people so is known as the second liberal and populous city in the whole of spain. It is one of the many most recognized cities of spain and is well known for its art, gastronomy and sports. Well known artists such as Pablo Picasso, Joan Miró, Antoni Tápies, Francisco Goya are all from spain and have created several artworks. Sports is a well known aspect of Barcelona as there are several sports that are most popular in this city. Sports such as soccer, handball, basketball, hockey, American football, tennis, surfing, rugby union, cycling, ice hockey, volleyball and bull fighting are the most popular sports played in barcelona. This city encompasses several historical sites with rich cultural heritage and plenty of magnificent architechtures, beaches and athlete events, these alluring places are the major tourist destinations in Barcelona which is very popular with tourists.
