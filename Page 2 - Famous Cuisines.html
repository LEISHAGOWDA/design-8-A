THE FAMOUS CUISINES OF BARCELONA

 Barcelona is famously known for several appetising dishes and cuisines present in the city, there are about 19 famous cuisines that are commonly tasted and tried by the tourists that visit this city. A few of the most popular cuisines of Barcelona are Crema Catalana, Pa amb tomàquet, Paella, Escalivada, Patatas Bravas, Fideuà, Esqueixada, Escudella, etc. These dishes are Barcelona's most popular and very delicious dishes.

THE MOST POPULAR CUISINES OF BARCELONA

 

 Crema Catalana a mediterranean dish is a spanish dessert that is the most famous dish or cuisine made throughout the region, Catalonia. The dessert is known in most of Spain as Crema Catalana is a custard made with milk, sugar, egg, cinnamon and lemon zest. All these ingredients are combined to prepare this most delectful and a sugary dish that is famous in the whole of Barcelona.

 

 Pa amb tomàquet is a traditional Catalan dish, Aragonese and Balearic cuisine that was invented in the 1800s and its place of origin is France and Spain. This dish is considered a staple of cuisine made out of bread, tomato, olive oil, garlic and salt.

 

Paella is one of the best-known dish in all of the spanish cuisines. It is a rice dish that is originally from Valencian community and was also the community's indentifying symbols. This cuisine with ancient roots that date back before the mid 19th century is made using rice, saffron, vegetables, chicken and seafood that gives it a wonderful taste.

 

 Escalivada in Spanish means Roast aubergine and peppers. Escalivada is a traditional Catalan dish from Roussillon Catalonia that is a warm salad of grilled vegetables like eggplants, bell, peppers, onions, tomatoes and olive oil. This dish is known for the taste of its smoky grilled vegetables.
 

Patatas Bravas is a native dish of Spain that are called as brave potatoes. They are one of the simplest and most popular Spanish tapas. Patatas Bravas are roasted potatoes coated with smoky tomato sauce with garlic, chilli and rosemary.
    Fideuà is a seafood dish originally from the coast of Valencia. It's main ingredients are pasta, noodles, fish and shellfish. This dish is like Paella(one of the famous dishes of Barcelona), but is made using pasta(main ingredient) instead of rice.
  Esqueixada is a quintessential Catalan salad, the perfect spring or summertime recipe. The main ingredient is always cod with tomatoes, onions, olive oil, vinegar, salt and green bell peppers.
  Escudella is a traditional Catalan and Valencian meat and vegetable stew and soup. It is an everyday winter dish in Catalonia and more elaborate versions were prepared at Christmas.
