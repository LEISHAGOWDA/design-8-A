ABOUT US

This is Leisha Gowda from grade 8 A studying at Sharanya Narayini International School, Thoranahalli Mallur, Kolar karnataka, India. I have create this website as a part of my Design E-Portfolio task, basically a project task, where I was asked to create a website on the topic of my choice. I created this website on, The Culture of Barcelona, a city in Spain.

This website aims to provide a simple guide for tourists who want to visit this city, this can also be used for research by educators as well as students. The goal is to provide information and to spread knowledge about a certain topic with the people around the audience.
